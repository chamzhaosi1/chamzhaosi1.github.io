WORK EXPERIENCE

Every profession produces its own topmost master

"A job should be from your interest or hobby because it will become the motivation to support us to handle everything and continuously improve our skills in the field". When I am a kid, I will always be heard about this statement. Although the statement is not false or true, some of the fields are not be included, or can be said that not easy to earn money to sustain our lives.

Some interest or hobby becomes a job may need to be postponed until we retire or we have extra income to make sure our lives. To be honest, doing a not interested job sometime is boring and suffering, but time and live is keep going.

Boring and suffering part-time job

Don't know why my family elder will ask their kids to do a part-time after graduated primary school, of course including my mother. As I 13 year old, I had to work as part time at the biscuit factory which nearly my house. At first a few week, I was curious the processing so everything is good. After a few month, I only feel boring and suffering, because I am doing repeat and repeat process for everyday. But this part-time is keep doing until my graduated secondary school.
