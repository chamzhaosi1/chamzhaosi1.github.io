TO BECOME A WED DEVELOPER

Junior Web Designer

As a junior web designer, there are many things that I have to learn and keep practicing. I may not able to do a perfect web design, but what only can I do is "Don't give up", because “Rome was not built in a day”. Actually, software engineering is not my first studying program after graduating from secondary school. I had achieved a certificate of ACCA diploma in accounting. At the time, I was not considering what will be my future and thought accounting will be a field that easy to find a job. Hence, I worked a related job for around 5 years in the same company. Just can be said, I was lucky to meet my kind colleague, grateful for her help and apologetic for my leaving.

Creating something is wonderful and amazing. It starts from zero until it has complete is a unforgettable processing. That is the reason why I further study and chose the course of software engineering. Although I am not that smart and creative, I believe the opportunity is for a person who is hard-working and prepared. UI / UX is difficult to measure and design if not that much experience. Therefore, by referring to a lot of great websites and understanding what layout and content are more user-friendly, then I will walk a step to close the goal.

AUTOBIOGRAPHY

Personal introduction

I was born in Muar Johor, I am the third child of my parents and I have an elder brother and a sister.

In my secondary school period, I studied in Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan (SMK) Sri Muar Sri Muar. I believe that is a wonderful period for everyone and including me. I joined some of the clubs and meet new friends. We participated in a few marching competitions and achieved well results, even though that was a very hard and tiring period.

However, it also is an unforgettable experience and period. Because I am not alone, there are many friends together to achieve the goal by our effort, sweat, and emotion is a very happy thing. After graduating, we are not gathered like previously, everyone has their own road and goal. Especially, when I know one of my friends has been married and has a baby, I am happy for them while sad for myself. Because they have stepped to the next field, but I even start yet. In conclusion, I just able to calm and talk to myself, "everything has been setted, just need to wait for the right moment".
